# Project Charter

## Vision

S3 InsightOS is the flagship AI-native research platform behind **S3conomics.ai**.

Our vision is to build an intelligence platform that helps researchers, product leaders, founders, investors, policymakers, and businesses understand how Artificial Intelligence is reshaping economies, industries, financial markets, labor, energy systems, and society.

Rather than building another AI chatbot, S3 InsightOS combines modern AI engineering with economics, systems thinking, and business strategy to generate evidence-backed insights from trusted knowledge sources.

The platform is being developed as both a production-grade AI application and a long-term learning journey in AI engineering, product management, and economic reasoning.
