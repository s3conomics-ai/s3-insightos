# S3 InsightOS

> **An AI-native research platform for understanding the economic, business, and societal impact of Artificial Intelligence.**

S3 InsightOS is the flagship technical platform behind **S3conomics.ai**.

It combines modern AI engineering with systems thinking, economics, financial markets, energy, and public policy to build an intelligent research platform capable of answering complex questions about how AI is transforming the world.

Unlike traditional AI applications that focus solely on chat or automation, S3 InsightOS is designed to reason across trusted research and produce structured, evidence-backed insights for researchers, founders, investors, product leaders, and policymakers.

This repository documents both the evolution of the platform and the engineering decisions behind it.
